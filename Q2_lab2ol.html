Ordered List

These are sometimes called numbered lists because,by default,the list items contained in that list have a specific numerical order or ranking. Ordered lists are appropriate where the exact ordering of items are important to the meaning of the content. An ordered list starts with the ol tag. Each list item starts with the li tag. The list items will be marked with numbers by default
